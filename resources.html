Step-by-Step Checklists

These checklists distill each phase of the incident response lifecycle into actionable steps. Available in PDF and Markdown formats.

  • Step 1: Prepare the Organization Building IR capabilities, team structure, communication plans, and tooling before an incident occurs.
    PDF Markdown
  • Step 2: Detect and Identify Detection strategies, alert triage, initial scoping, and incident classification.
    PDF Markdown
  • Step 3: Contain the Incident Containment strategies, network isolation, account actions, and evidence preservation during containment.
    PDF Markdown
  • Step 4: Eradicate the Threat Removing attacker access, cleaning compromised systems, and verifying eradication completeness.
    PDF Markdown
  • Step 5: Recover Operations Restoring systems, validating recovery, and transitioning back to normal operations.
